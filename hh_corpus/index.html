Hybrid Hashtag Sub-Corpus

The Hybrid Hashtag Sub-Corpus (HH Corpus) is a subset of tweets in the MLT corpus containing hashtags made up of M훮ori and English words (so-called "hybrid hashtags"). There are 81 hybrid hashtags in this dataset, used in 5,684 tweets and posted to Twitter by 3,771 distinct users.
